# Caffeine and alertness

Caffeine is the most widely consumed psychoactive substance in the world. It
works primarily by blocking adenosine receptors in the brain, which reduces the
feeling of sleepiness and increases subjective alertness.

Moderate caffeine intake can improve reaction time and sustained attention,
particularly in people who are tired. However, caffeine consumed late in the day
can delay sleep onset and reduce total sleep time, which may in turn affect next
day performance.
